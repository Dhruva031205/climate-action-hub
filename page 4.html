Climate Action Hub Basics Causes of Climate Change Effects of Climate Change Global Initiatives

There are many solutions for stopping climate change and many are in affect right now. The largest factor contributing to this is damage to the enviorenment. Not recycling recycable items is a massive problem as well as litter and the usage of cars. Older manual cars emit so much carbon dioxide per journey that really harms the atmosphere and has its effect on the enviorenment. However, electric cars are being manufactured more and more as time goes on which has a much better effect on the enviorenment, water is starting to be produced in cartons as opposed to bottles to help with the recycling issue and bottle caps now stay attached to the bottle after being opened which reduces people littering them.
